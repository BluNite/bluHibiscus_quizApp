Rules of The Quiz

The timer is set for 40 seconds. Each correct answer is worth 10 points. A bonus of 5 points is added to player score after reaching 20 points. "Incorrect" answers will decrease players score by 3 points, and a 10 point time penalty for each response. Score includes time remaining which is added at the end of the game.

To select an answer, point and click on the choice text.

A "Correct" response will display a green background and its answer, and an "Incorrect" response will yield a red background on the chosen answer. At the bottom of the game box, "Incorrect" will appear alongside the "Correct" response. At the end of the quiz enter your user name to track ranked high scores.
